About

The Idea:

M0T1V8R was made to help you keep up with goals you want to complete on the daily, such as: flossing your teeth, exercising, eating healthy, etc.

What we hope to accomplish:

We hope to help you get into the habit of accomplishing your goals so that you won't need to use M0T1V8R.


APIs


We Use Twilio

We use Twilio to send you texts reminding you of your goals. (Message and data rates may apply).

Firebase Authentication

We use Firebase to let our users log in and track their progress.

BootStrap

Our website use the Bootstrap 4 CSS framework. It is also mobile friendly.
