About

The Idea:

M0T1V8R was made to help you keep up with goals you want to complete, such as: flossing your teeth, exercising daily, eating healthy, etc.


APIs

We use Twilio

We use Twilio to send you texts reminding you of your goals. (Message and data rates may apply).

Firebase Authentication

We use Firebase to let our users log in and track their progress.

Bootstrap 4

Our website use the Bootstrap 4 CSS framework. It is also mobile friendly.
